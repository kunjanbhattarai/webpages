About Me

Passionate educator, developer, and finance enthusiast with a drive for innovation and social impact

Hello! I'm Kunjan Bhattarai, a dedicated educator and technology enthusiast currently pursuing my BBS degree. With a strong foundation in teaching and development, I strive to create engaging learning experiences and innovative digital solutions.

I have extensive teaching experience at Oasis Institute for Class 10 students, where I've also developed marketing strategies to enhance institutional growth. My volunteer work at Trinity International Secondary School further solidified my passion for education.

My interests span across education, technology, and financial markets. I'm particularly fascinated by the Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE) and actively engage in trading and market analysis. My financial knowledge was expanded through a comprehensive 3-month entrepreneur course that provided deep insights into business strategies and financial management.
