About Me

Get to know more about who I am and what I do

Hello! I'm Kunjan Bhattarai, a passionate educator, developer, and finance enthusiast currently pursuing my BBS degree. With a strong background in teaching and technology, I strive to make learning accessible and engaging for everyone.

I have teaching experience at Orbit Institute for Class 10 students and have also volunteered at Trinity International Secondary School. Beyond academics, I'm actively involved in social work through organizations like Leo and Lions clubs.
